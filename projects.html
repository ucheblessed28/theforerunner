2019 Outreach Programs

As a non Governmental Organization (NGO) the forerunners foundation international seeks to give hope and succour to widows and orphans, give direction to many who have lost their ways and mobilize indigent youth through education for active participation in the social process. These goals seek to we achieve through our various outreaches, training programmes and workshops.
