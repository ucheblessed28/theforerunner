The Forerunner Foundation

A non-profit, non-partisan organization founded and entrenched in the ideals of Christian Charity for the empowerment of the less privileged in society and youth regeneration.

As a non Governmental Organization (NGO) the forerunners foundation international seeks to give hope and succour to widows and orphans, give direction to many who have lost their ways and mobilize indigent youth through education for active participation in the social process. These goals seek to we achieve through our various outreaches, training programmes and workshops.
The chief visioners Reverend Marius & Evang. (Mrs.) Carol Alugwe are seasoned Servants of God and the State Co-ordinator of Christian Pentecostal Mission (CPM), Rivers State.

Who We Are

In a world filled with fear, poverty and disease - where ignorance and deprivation dehumanizes men and women alike, we are persuaded that God can count on you to bring hope to the hopeless, direction to the confused and succour to the downtrodden.

Our Vision

To create a veritable platform for the transformation of lives of the less privileged in society.

To give hope to the hopeless, through scholarship programmes to children of indigent members of society.

Empower widows through skills acquisition, training and provision of soft loan for business.

Cater for victims of all forms of disasters including natural ones.

Play a pivotal role in the nurturing of our future leaders

Our Mission

Use our outreaches to communities to identify widows, orphans and destitutes that are deserving of assistance and through our various programmes impact these lives positively for effective participation in the society, empower indigent persons through training and skills acquisition to became productive members of society, minister Christ love and care to victims of disasters, nurture and groom the upcoming leaders on sound principles of leadership.

Forerunners foundation international is made up of a team of highly dedicated, resourceful and dynamic professionals, men & women who have uncommon passion for humanity and a relentless urge to reach out and minister to the lost based on the exemplary teachings of our Lord and Saviour, Jesus Christ.
